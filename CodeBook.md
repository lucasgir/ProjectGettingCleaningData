The tidy dataset contains two main part :
* Dimension

    * Value 1 to 30
  * Activity : The type of activity the subject was doing
    * Walking
    * Walking upstairs
    * Walking downstaires
    * Sitting
    * Standing
    * Laying
*Measures
    * mean
    * standard deviation
